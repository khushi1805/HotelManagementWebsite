Style and Comfort for your stay.

Every room or suite of Lilium Santorini Hotel is a spacious and fully renovated accommodation with a private or semi- private balcony with sea, volcano and sunset views so that you can enjoy every moment of your visit in Santorini!

Standard room with Caldera View



Occupancy for 3 guests with one large double bed and 1 single bed. Free Wi-Fi, flat screen tv, wardrobe, fridge, pool towel, electric kettle, coffee machine.

Pool level room with Caldera View



Occupancy for upto 3 guests with one large double bed and 1 single bed on pool level. Free Wi-Fi, flat screen tv, wardrobe, fridge, pool towel, electric kettle, coffee machine.

Suite with Caldera View



Occupancy for upto 4 guests with one large double bed and 2 sofa beds. Free Wi-Fi, flat screen tv, wardrobe, fridge, pool towel, electric kettle, coffee machine. Room also has its private balcony.

Superior double room with Caldera View



Occupancy for 2 guests with one large double bed. Free Wi-Fi, flat screen tv, wardrobe, fridge, pool towel, electric kettle, coffee machine. Room also has its private balcony with a Hot tub.

Lilium Suite



Occupancy for 2 guests with one large double bed. Free Wi-Fi, flat screen tv, wardrobe, fridge, pool towel, electric kettle, coffee machine. Room with private balcony and a private pool.

Lilium Grand Suite



Occupancy for 2 guests with one king double bed and sofa. Free Wi-Fi, flat screen tv, wardrobe, fridge, pool towel, electric kettle, coffee machine. Room has a private balcony and a terrace with hot tub.
